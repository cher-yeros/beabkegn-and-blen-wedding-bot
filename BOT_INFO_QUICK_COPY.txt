PRIVACY POLICY - Abela & Hanich Wedding Bot

Last Updated: January 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION
We collect the following information when you use this bot:
- Your Telegram User ID (automatically provided by Telegram)
- Messages you send to us through the bot
- Photos you share with us
- Reminder preferences you set

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
- To forward your messages and photos to the wedding couple (admin)
- To store your reminder preferences
- To improve the bot's functionality

3. DATA STORAGE
- All data is stored locally on our server
- Messages and photos are saved in JSON files
- Your Telegram User ID is used only for bot functionality

4. DATA SHARING
- Your messages and photos are forwarded to the wedding couple (admin) only
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not sell your personal information

5. YOUR RIGHTS
- You can stop using the bot at any time
- You can request information about your stored data
- Contact us if you have privacy concerns

6. SECURITY
- We take reasonable measures to protect your data
- Data is stored securely on our server
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

7. CONTACT
For privacy concerns or questions, please contact the bot administrator through Telegram.

By using this bot, you agree to this privacy policy.
